Meet Vaasanthika

Clinical Psychologist (MSc) · Founder of Heartsease

Confidential · Evidence-informed · Client-centred

I work with people who feel overwhelmed, stuck in repetitive thought patterns, or emotionally exhausted by life’s demands.

My work focuses on understanding emotions, identifying unhelpful thinking patterns, and building practical strategies that support long-term psychological well-being.

Therapy with me isn’t just about understanding your past — it’s about developing clarity, resilience, and tools that help you navigate your future with confidence.
