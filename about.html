Hi, I'm Vaasanthika.

Clinical Psychologist (MSc) · Founder of Heartsease Counseling Services

Confidential · Trauma-informed · Nervous-system-focused

I created Heartsease from a simple but deeply personal belief: that healing doesn't happen through force, productivity, or constant self-fixing — it happens through safety, understanding, and consistent emotional care.

Over the years, I've worked with individuals who look "fine" on the outside but feel exhausted, disconnected, anxious, or stuck on the inside. People who are self-aware, capable, and strong — yet tired of surviving instead of living.

My Approach

Therapy that goes beyond talking

My approach to therapy is trauma-informed and nervous-system-focused. That means we don't just talk about what you're feeling — we work with how your body and mind learned to protect you in the first place.

Together, we slow things down, build emotional regulation, unpack relationship patterns, and create change that actually lasts.

My work is shaped by lived experience, continuous learning, and the belief that therapy should feel human, collaborative, and grounding — not intimidating or clinical.

Heartsease is growing into a space where multiple therapists will offer aligned, ethical, and compassionate care under one shared philosophy — prioritising emotional safety, clarity, and respectful healing.
