Before You Begin

What to Expect From Therapy

Starting therapy can feel uncertain — especially if you've never done it before.

This page is here to help you know what therapy with me looks like, feels like, and doesn't expect from you.

There's no pressure to have the right words.
You just need to show up as you are.

About Sessions

01 How do I prepare for my first therapy session?

Once you book a session:

  • You'll receive a confirmation with session details
  • Sessions are held online in a private, secure setting
  • You don't need to prepare anything in advance

You don't need a "reason big enough." If something feels heavy, confusing, or unresolved — that's enough.

02 What can I expect during a session?

Therapy is a conversation — but not the kind where you're judged, rushed, or analysed. In sessions, you can expect:

  • A calm, confidential space
  • Time to speak freely, at your pace
  • Gentle questions that help you reflect
  • Support that feels human, not clinical

There's no pressure to talk nonstop. Silence is welcome too.

03 What will we be working on?

Therapy isn't about fixing you — it's about understanding you. Depending on what you're coming in for, we may:

  • Explore emotional patterns and triggers
  • Understand how your past affects your present
  • Learn practical tools for regulation and clarity
  • Work on communication, boundaries, or self-trust
  • Slowly move you out of survival mode

Everything is adapted to you. There's no one-size-fits-all approach here.

04 What therapy is not

To set honest expectations, therapy is not:

  • A place where you're told what to do
  • A space where you're rushed to "heal faster"
  • A judgement zone or a performance test
  • A quick fix or motivational talk

Therapy is a process. And it works best when it's gentle, consistent, and collaborative.

05 How can I tell if therapy is working for me?

Progress in therapy doesn't always look dramatic. Sometimes it looks like:

  • Responding instead of reacting
  • Feeling calmer in situations that once overwhelmed you
  • Understanding yourself with less self-blame
  • Making decisions with more clarity
  • Feeling safer in your own mind

Some sessions feel light. Some feel heavy. Both are part of the work.

Scheduling & Logistics

06 How often are sessions?

Most clients start with weekly sessions, especially in the beginning. Over time, this may shift depending on your needs and comfort.

Therapy moves at your pace, not a fixed timeline.

07 How long is each therapy session?

Each session is 45–50 minutes long.

08 How do I schedule an appointment?

Click the Book a Session button on the homepage. After filling in some basic details, you can choose your preferred slot and confirm with your preferred method of payment.

09 Can I schedule an appointment online?

Yes. You can book an appointment by clicking on the Book a Session button. After booking, you will receive a confirmation email with all session details.

10 How soon can I get an appointment?

The earliest available slot depends on current demand. To check current availability, click the Book a Session button and browse the date strip for open slots.

Still have questions? I'm happy to answer them before you book.
